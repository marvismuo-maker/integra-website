### About (Description)
Integra Research & Business Solutions (杭州合研商策科技有限公司) is a medical manuscript preparation and publication support company registered in Hangzhou, China, serving researchers worldwide.

Founded by Marvis Muo (RPh), a pharmacist and clinical research associate with 50+ peer-reviewed publications, 100+ peer reviews, and 10+ ghostwritten books & textbooks, Integra exists to solve the single biggest problem facing researchers in low-resource settings: getting published in legitimate, indexed journals without predatory traps or unaffordable fees.

**Our Hard Constraints:**
- APC ≤ $150
- Review time ≤ 5 weeks
- 100% acceptance rate to date
- DOAJ / PubMed / Scopus indexed only
- Zero predatory journals

**Services:**
- Manuscript Preparation & Submission (10-phase IMRAD pipeline)
- Journal Selection & Verification
- Statistical Audit & Data Integrity
- Publication Proposal Development
- Conference Presentation Design
- Procurement Documentation
- Book & Textbook Ghostwriting

We believe that where you practice shouldn't determine whether your research gets seen. A researcher in Lagos, Hangzhou, or Nairobi deserves the same publication opportunities as one in London or New York. We make that possible.
