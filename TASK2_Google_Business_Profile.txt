## SERVICES LIST

### 1. Medical Manuscript Preparation
Full 10-phase pipeline from raw data to submission-ready manuscript. IMRAD structure, statistical integrity checks, ethical compliance, and cover letter drafting. Includes internal peer review before submission.

### 2. Journal Selection & Verification
We maintain a verified bank of journals meeting strict criteria: APC ≤$150, review time ≤5 weeks, indexed in DOAJ/PubMed/Scopus, and verified non-predatory. Every project gets a 3-tier journal cascade.

### 3. Statistical Analysis & Data Audit
Pre-submission audit of your data analysis for accuracy, reproducibility, and presentation quality. Includes test verification, figure preparation, and missing data assessment.

### 4. Publication Proposal Development
Transform your dissertation or thesis into 3-5 publishable papers with strategic journal cascades, timelines, and authorship frameworks. Ideal for PhD graduates with unpublished work.

### 5. Conference Presentation Design
30-slide evidence-based keynotes for senior clinicians. Branded templates, speaker notes, rehearsal guides, and publication-ready handouts.

### 6. Procurement Documentation
Professional contracts, quotations, and procurement documents for research projects and vendor engagements. Branded, compliant, and signature-ready.

## FAQ SECTION (5 Questions)

**Q1: What types of manuscripts do you prepare?**
A: We handle original research, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, case reports, survey studies, and dissertation-to-publication conversions across medicine, pharmacy, nursing, and allied health.

**Q2: How fast can I get published?**
A: Manuscript preparation takes 7-14 days. Our fastest client was accepted in 6 days at IJRISS with $0 APC. Most papers receive first decisions within 2-5 weeks.

**Q3: What journals do you work with?**
A: Only verified journals with APCs under $150, review times under 5 weeks, and indexing in DOAJ, PubMed, or Scopus. We never recommend predatory journals.

**Q4: How much do your services cost?**
A: Manuscript preparation ranges from ₦150,000–₦300,000. Journal selection is ₦30,000. We also offer a free initial publication analysis with no obligation.

**Q5: Do you work with researchers outside Port Harcourt?**
A: Absolutely. We serve researchers across all of Nigeria — Lagos, Kano, Calabar, Sokoto, everywhere. All services are delivered remotely.
