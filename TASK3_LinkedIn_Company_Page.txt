## ABOUT SECTION (Full Version — 2,000+ characters)

Integra Research and Business Solutions is a publication support firm dedicated to helping Nigerian medical researchers get their work into indexed, peer-reviewed journals — quickly, affordably, and ethically.

Founded by Marvis Muo, a pharmacist and research specialist with 6 published papers and a 100% acceptance rate, Integra was built on one belief: where you practice shouldn't determine whether your research gets seen.

We serve doctors, pharmacists, nurses, public health professionals, and allied health researchers across Nigeria. All our services are delivered remotely, so whether you're in Lagos, Kano, Calabar, or Sokoto, you have the same access to world-class publication support.

**OUR SERVICES:**

📝 Medical Manuscript Preparation — Full 10-phase pipeline from data to submission-ready manuscript. IMRAD structure, statistical integrity, ethical compliance.
