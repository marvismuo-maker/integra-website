### 1. Manuscript Preparation & Submission
**Price:** ₦150,000 – ₦300,000
**Description:** Full 10-phase pipeline from your research data to submission-ready manuscript. Includes IMRAD structuring, statistical integrity checks, ethical compliance review, cover letter drafting, internal peer review, and complete submission handling via journal portals (ScholarOne, Editorial Manager, eJournalPress). You get a publication-ready manuscript + journal recommendation report + submission confirmation.

### 2. Journal Selection & Verification
**Price:** ₦30,000
**Description:** We identify 3 verified journals matched to your research topic, meeting our strict criteria: APC ≤$150, review time ≤5 weeks, indexed in DOAJ/PubMed/Scopus, and confirmed non-predatory. Includes a 3-tier cascade strategy (primary, secondary, tertiary targets) with full journal profiles, APCs, acceptance rates, and submission requirements.

### 3. Statistical Audit & Data Integrity Check
**Price:** ₦50,000 – ₦100,000
**Description:** Pre-submission verification of your data analysis including statistical test appropriateness, figure and table formatting, reproducibility documentation, missing data assessment, outlier handling review, and power analysis. Deliverable: audit report + corrected analysis + publication-ready figures.

### 4. Publication Proposal (Dissertation Conversion)
**Price:** ₦75,000
**Description:** Transform your completed dissertation or thesis into 3-5 publishable papers. Includes chapter-to-paper mapping, scoping review, novelty assessment, 3-tier journal cascade design for each paper, 12-month timeline, and authorship framework. Ideal for PhD graduates and research fellows with unpublished work.

### 5. Conference Presentation Design
**Price:** ₦60,000
**Description:** 30-slide evidence-based keynote or presentation for senior clinicians and researchers. Includes audience-adaptive content, evidence hierarchy visualization, branded slide templates (navy/gold), speaker notes, rehearsal guide, and publication-ready handout PDF.
