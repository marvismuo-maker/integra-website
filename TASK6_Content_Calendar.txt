## CONTENT CREATION TIPS

### LinkedIn Best Practices
- Post between 8-10 AM WAT (Nigerian morning commute)
- Use 3-5 hashtags max, mixing broad (#MedicalResearch) and niche (#NigerianResearchers)
- Tag relevant institutions when appropriate (RSUTH, UNIPORT, etc.)
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours
- Pin the founder introduction post to your profile

### ResearchGate Best Practices
- Post on Monday/Wednesday/Friday mornings
- Share full papers when ethically possible (with co-author permission)
- Ask specific methodological questions to drive engagement
- Update publications list weekly

### X/Twitter Best Practices
- Keep tweets under 280 characters
- Use threads for longer content
- Quote-tweet relevant journal announcements
- Engage with #AcademicTwitter and #MedTwitter conversations
- Post Tuesday/Thursday/Saturday evenings

### WhatsApp Status Best Practices
- Use photo + text (not text-only)
- Show behind-the-scenes content (desk, laptop, research papers)
- Post Wednesday and Saturday evenings
- Keep text short (2-3 lines max)
- Always include contact method

### Google Business Profile Best Practices
- Post Monday mornings
- Include local keywords ("Port Harcourt", "Nigeria")
- Add photos when possible
- Use action buttons (Call Now, Learn More)
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
