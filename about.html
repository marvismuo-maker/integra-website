The Story Behind Integra

I started Integra Research & Business Solutions after experiencing firsthand how difficult and expensive it is for researchers worldwide to get published. I spent months navigating journal selection, manuscript preparation, and submission portals — only to discover that most "publication support" services charge fees that make publication impossible for researchers in low-resource settings.

So I built something different: a service that understands the global research context, respects your budget, and delivers results. Fifty-plus publications later — all accepted, all in indexed journals, all under $150 APC — plus ghostwritten books and textbooks for academic and professional audiences — I've proven that world-class publication support doesn't require world-class budgets.

My Background

I'm a pharmacist by training, a researcher by passion, and a publication specialist by experience. Beyond 50+ peer-reviewed publications with 100% acceptance, I've ghostwritten books and textbooks for academic and professional audiences — translating complex medical research into accessible, authoritative content. I've counseled over 25,000 clients with zero errors, managed ₦30 million in monthly pharmaceutical inventory with 98% accuracy, and led procurement processes that won ₦45 million in bids with an 80% success rate.

This same precision and reliability now powers every manuscript we prepare at Integra.

Our Mission

To help researchers worldwide — in medicine, pharmacy, nursing, and allied health — publish their work in legitimate, indexed journals quickly, affordably, and without the predatory traps that too many fall into.

We believe that where you practice shouldn't determine whether your research gets seen. A researcher in Hangzhou deserves the same publication opportunities as one in London or New York. We make that possible.

Where We Work

Based in Hangzhou, China, we serve researchers worldwide — from Africa to Asia, Europe to the Americas. All our services are delivered remotely, so your location is never a barrier.
