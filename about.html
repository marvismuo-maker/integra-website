Who We Are

Integra Research & Business Solutions (IRBS) is a dual-division consultancy founded on one principle: that world-class expertise should be accessible to professionals everywhere, regardless of location or budget.

We operate two specialized branches that stand side by side, each serving distinct but complementary needs:

Integra Research

Our research division provides end-to-end publication support for medical and academic researchers. From manuscript preparation to journal submission, we ensure your research gets published in indexed journals — fast, affordable, and with 100% acceptance.

We understand the global research context, respect your budget constraints, and deliver results. Fifty-plus publications later — all accepted, all in indexed journals, all under $150 APC — we've proven that world-class publication support doesn't require world-class budgets.

Integra Business Solutions

Our business division provides strategic procurement and supply chain consulting for healthcare institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and government agencies. We optimize costs, win bids, and manage inventory with precision.

With an 80% bid win rate and ₦45 million in contracts secured, we bring the same rigor and reliability to procurement that we bring to publication support.

Our Mission

To help professionals worldwide — in medicine, pharmacy, nursing, allied health, and healthcare procurement — achieve excellence quickly, affordably, and without the barriers that too many face.

We believe that where you practice shouldn't determine whether your research gets seen or your procurement succeeds. A professional in Enugu deserves the same opportunities as one in London or New York. We make that possible.

Where We Work

With offices in Enugu, Nigeria and Hangzhou, China, we serve clients worldwide — from Africa to Asia, Europe to the Americas. All our services are delivered remotely, so your location is never a barrier.
