Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Integra

What types of manuscripts do you handle? ▼
We specialize in medical and health sciences research, including: original research articles, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, case reports and case series, cross-sectional and survey studies, and dissertation-to-publication conversions. We primarily serve medicine, pharmacy, nursing, public health, and allied health fields. If you have a manuscript outside these areas, contact us — we may still be able to help.
How fast can you get my paper published? ▼
From the moment you send us your data, our typical timeline is: Manuscript preparation: 7-14 days depending on complexity; Journal selection: 2-3 days; Submission to first decision: 2-5 weeks (based on our verified journal bank). Our fastest result: Dr. Collins Ordu's paper was accepted in 6 days at IJRISS with $0 APC. We also offer an expedited service for urgent submissions — ask about it when you request your free analysis.
What journals do you work with? ▼
We maintain a verified bank of journals that meet four strict criteria: (1) APC ≤ $150 (₦225,000), (2) Review time ≤ 5 weeks to first decision, (3) Indexed in DOAJ, PubMed, or Scopus, and (4) Not predatory — verified via Beall's List, DOAJ, and Cabells. We don't work with journals that fail any of these tests. For every project, we provide a 3-tier journal cascade: a primary target, a secondary fallback, and a tertiary option. This maximizes your chances of acceptance without wasting time.
What are your fees? ▼
We offer transparent, flat-fee pricing with no hidden costs: Manuscript Preparation & Submission: ₦150,000 – ₦300,000 depending on complexity; Journal Selection Only: ₦30,000; Statistical Audit: ₦50,000 – ₦100,000; Publication Proposal (Dissertation): ₦75,000; Conference Presentation: ₦60,000; Procurement Documentation: ₦40,000. All journal APCs are paid separately by you directly to the journal — we never mark these up. We also offer a free initial publication analysis with no obligation.
Do you guarantee acceptance? ▼
No ethical publication service can guarantee acceptance — and you should be wary of any that do. What we guarantee is: (1) a publication-ready manuscript that meets journal standards, (2) a verified, appropriate journal match, (3) a professional submission with all requirements met, and (4) support through revisions if reviewers request changes. Our current acceptance rate is 100% — but this reflects our rigorous journal matching and preparation process, not a promise we can't keep. If your paper is rejected, we help you revise and resubmit to the next journal in your cascade at no extra charge.
What if my paper is rejected? ▼
Rejection is a normal part of academic publishing — even Nobel Prize winners get rejected. If your paper is rejected, here's what happens: (1) We analyze the reviewer feedback (if provided) and revise the manuscript accordingly; (2) We identify the next best journal from your pre-approved cascade; (3) We resubmit at no additional service fee. We repeat this process until your paper is accepted. This is why our 3-tier journal cascade is so important — it gives you multiple paths to publication without starting over.
Can you work with my co-authors? ▼
Absolutely. We regularly work with multi-author teams and can: coordinate contributions from all authors, manage author order and affiliation formatting, handle corresponding author preferences, and ensure all co-authors approve the final manuscript before submission. We also help resolve common authorship disputes by referring to ICMJE guidelines. Just let us know your team structure when you submit your request.
Do you handle the submission process? ▼
Yes — completely. We handle the entire submission process including: creating or managing author accounts on journal portals (ScholarOne, Editorial Manager, eJournalPress), formatting the manuscript to journal-specific guidelines, writing the cover letter, uploading all required documents (manuscript, figures, tables, supplementary materials, ethical approval, conflict of interest forms), and tracking the submission status. You stay informed at every step, but you don't have to wrestle with submission portals.
What do you need from me to start? ▼
To get started, we need: (1) Your research data — dissertation, thesis, raw data, or a detailed topic description; (2) Your target journal preferences (if any) or your field of study; (3) Author information — names, affiliations, ORCID IDs, contact details; (4) Ethical approvals — IRB/ethics committee approval numbers (for human/animal research); (5) Funding and conflict of interest disclosures. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we guide you through what's needed during our free analysis call.
Is my research data confidential? ▼
Completely. We treat your research data with the same confidentiality as a medical record. Our commitments: (1) We never share your data with third parties; (2) We never publish or present your work without your explicit written consent; (3) We store files securely and delete them 30 days after project completion (unless you request otherwise); (4) We can sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) if your institution requires it; (5) All co-authors retain full intellectual property rights. Your research is yours — we just help you publish it.
