📝 Manuscript Preparation & Submission

Our flagship 10-phase pipeline takes your research data from raw input to submission-ready manuscript. We follow IMRAD structure, ensure statistical integrity, and handle ethical compliance documentation.

  • Phase 1-2: Data intake & scope definition
  • Phase 3-4: Literature gap analysis & methodology design
  • Phase 5-6: Full manuscript draft with IMRAD structure
  • Phase 7-8: Internal peer review & revision
  • Phase 9-10: Journal matching & submission execution

🔍 Journal Selection & Verification

We maintain a verified bank of journals that meet our strict criteria. Every journal we recommend is vetted for legitimacy, indexing, and affordability.

  • Hard Constraint — APC: Maximum $150 (₦225,000)
  • Hard Constraint — Review Time: Maximum 5 weeks to first decision
  • Hard Constraint — Indexing: DOAJ, PubMed, or Scopus minimum
  • Hard Constraint — Ethics: No predatory journals
  • Cascade Strategy: 3-tier fallback plan for every submission

Deliverable: Verified journal shortlist with APCs, review timelines, acceptance rates, and submission requirements.
